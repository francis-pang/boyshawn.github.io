User Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: February 8, 2026

How to Delete Your Data

If you would like to delete the data associated with your account on the Miniature Disco Facebook application, you can do so using any of the following methods:

Option 1: Remove the App from Facebook

  1. Log in to your Facebook account
  2. Go to Settings & Privacy > Settings
  3. Select Apps and Websites from the left menu
  4. Find Miniature Disco in the list of active apps
  5. Click Remove next to the app
  6. Check the box for Delete all posts, photos, and videos on Facebook that Miniature Disco may have published on your behalf
  7. Click Remove

All data associated with your account will be deleted upon removal.

Option 2: Contact Us Directly

If you are unable to remove the app through Facebook settings, you can request data deletion by opening an issue in this repository. Please include:

  • The email address associated with your Facebook account
  • A brief description of your request

We will process your request and delete all associated data within 30 days.

What Data Is Deleted

When you request data deletion, we remove all information linked to your account, including:

  • Profile information (name, profile picture)
  • Email address
  • Any stored session or authentication data

Confirmation

Once your data has been deleted, you will no longer appear in our system. If you removed the app via Facebook, no further action is needed.
