Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Miniature Disco ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Facebook application.

Information We Collect

When you use our app, we may collect the following information from your Facebook account:

  • Public profile information (name, profile picture)
  • Email address (if granted permission)

We only collect information that is necessary for the app to function.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the app
  • Authenticate your identity
  • Improve and maintain the app

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following cases:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or safety

Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide the app's functionality. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Data Deletion

You can remove the app's access to your data by visiting your Facebook App Settings and removing the app. Upon removal, we will delete all stored data associated with your account.

For full instructions, see our User Data Deletion page.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
